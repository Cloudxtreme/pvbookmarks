Why ?
======

Let's join together to gather all human knowledge and let every person 
in the world free access to it.

Sounds inconceivable? That's what we're doing.

And we need your help.

Our Mission
===========

Wikimedia Israel is an Israeli non-profit organization working in 
cooperation with the the international Wikimedia Foundation. 

The organization is working to promote knowledge and education in 
Israel by gathering, creating and distributing free content.
