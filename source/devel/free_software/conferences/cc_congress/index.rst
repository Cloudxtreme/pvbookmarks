====================================
Chaos Communication Congress (1984-
====================================

.. seealso::

   - http://www.ccc.de/de/
   - http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chaos_Communication_Congress
   - http://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chaos_Communication_Congress


Depuis 1984, le CCC tient tous les ans un Chaos Communication Congress, qui a
lieu habituellement autour de Noël à Berlin.

The event has taken place regularly at the end of the year since 1984.
A fixed time frame (December 27 to December 30) has been established for a
couple of years now.

The Congress, having grown up in Hamburg, moved to Berlin in 1998, and back to
Hamburg in 2012.

It usually attracts between 2,500 and 3,500 participants.

The 2008 meeting had a record attendance of 4,230.
