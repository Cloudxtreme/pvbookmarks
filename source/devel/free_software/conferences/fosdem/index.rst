Presentation
============

FOSDEM is the biggest free and non-commercial event organized by and for the
community. Its goal is to provide Free and Open Source developers a place to
meet. No registration necessary.

La FOSDEM (Free and Open Source Software Developers' European Meeting),
réunion européenne des développeurs de logiciels libres et open source)
est un ensemble de conférences qui se déroulent annuellement pendant un week-end.
