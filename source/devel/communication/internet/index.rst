========
Internet
========

.. seealso::

   - https://scm.info.ucl.ac.be/release/cnp3/Book/0.1/html/intro/introduction.html
   - http://inl.info.ucl.ac.be/cnp3
   - http://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet
   - http://www.internetactu.net/2013/03/21/pouvoir-et-internet/


The Internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks that use the
standard Internet Protocol Suite (TCP/IP) to serve billions of users worldwide.
It is a network of networks that consists of millions of private, public,
academic, business, and government networks, of local to global scope, that are
linked by a broad array of electronic, wireless and optical networking technologies
