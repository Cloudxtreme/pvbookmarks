==================
ISO 15693 protocol
==================

.. seealso::

   - http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_15693
   - http://www.waazaa.org/15693/

ISO 15693 is an international standard for "Vicinity Cards", i.e. contactless
cards operating at the 13.56 MHz frequency, with a maximum operating distance
of 1 to 1.5 metres.
