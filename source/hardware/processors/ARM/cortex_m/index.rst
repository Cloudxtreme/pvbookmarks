ARM Cortex M

.. seealso::

Introduction
============

The ARM Cortex-M is a group of 32-bit RISC ARM processor cores licensed 
by :ref:`ARM Holdings <arm_holdings>`. 

The cores are intended for microcontroller applications, and consists of 
the:

- Cortex-M0, 
- Cortex-M0+, 
- Cortex-M1, 
- Cortex-M3, 
- Cortex-M4.
