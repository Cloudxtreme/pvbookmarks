.. seealso::

Introduction
============

AT91SAM (AT91 Smart ARM Microcontrollers) is a family of microcontroller 
ICs by Atmel. 

The AT91SAM chips are based on the 32-bit RISC ARM cores from ARM Holdings, 
such as Cortex-M4, Cortex-M3, Cortex-A5, ARM9, and ARM7 cores
